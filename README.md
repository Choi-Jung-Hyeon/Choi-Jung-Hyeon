## 🧭 About Me

> **"The ultimate purpose of technology is to make people's everyday lives more convenient and safe."**

I'm a Computer Science student at **Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU)** and a **full-cycle developer** who works across **planning, design, frontend, backend, infrastructure, and AI**. My biggest strength is that I'm not confined to a single role — I can participate in every stage of shipping a product. I aim to build **solid, warm services that anyone can use easily**, rather than showing off flashy tech.

- 🧩 **Full-Cycle** — hands-on experience from user research & product specs to UI/UX design, FE/BE implementation, and infrastructure setup
- 🏦 **Samsung Securities YoungCreator (15th)** — 3 finance × AI projects: a risk-report engine, an OpenCode-integrated MCP (Model Context Protocol) server, and a VVIP asset-advisor hub
- ⚖️ **SKKUDING** — Backend/Frontend engineer for [Codedang](https://github.com/skkuding/codedang), SKKU's Online Judge platform; member of the infra study group
- 🔬 **Industry-Academia Project with LG AI Research (Team Lead)** — research on training-free detection of AI-generated images
- 🧠 **Undergraduate Researcher, SKKU NLP Lab** — DPO/GRPO paper seminars, RAG & LLM pipelines, persona-data preprocessing
- 🏗️ **SW Internship at Hyscape** — physically set up air-gapped NAS infrastructure and launched an in-house data-automation pipeline with LLM API integration
- 🎓 **Graduation Project** — a [stock-prediction model & web platform](https://github.com/2026Graduation-Work/Stock_Prediction_v2) reflecting behavioral-finance sentiment indices
- 🌐 **English** — OPIc Intermediate High; comfortable collaborating and interviewing in English

## 💡 How I Work

- **Plan first** — I analyze user-centered requirements and document them before writing code. Clear planning determines development speed.
- **Bridge between roles** — Backend is my main position, but I understand design, frontend, and infra workflows well enough to align API contracts between teams and remove bottlenecks proactively.
- **Failure is growth data** — Facing error logs and hardware constraints, I choose deep focus on problem-solving over frustration.
- **Solution-oriented code reviews** — Beyond pointing out issues, I always propose actionable technical alternatives.
